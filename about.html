Hi there! I'm Jess. I'm an Experince Design Director and Animator interested in creating technically complex but soulful experiences through a variety of mediums ranging from experimental film and animation to digital products and physical installations.

Currently, I'm a Senior Product Designer at Disney, where I build features for content engagement and discovery through unique streaming experiences.

I'm also the Writer and Experience Director on Doors We Open, an interactive film, with my collaborators Kevin Peter He and David Woon. We are currently in production, and plan to premiere early 2022.

Previously I was an Experience Design Director at R/GA New York, where I led and built interactive experiences for global clients. I had the opportunity to work abroad for a year, creating culturally driven global products in Shanghai and Singapore. I recently spoke about the experience at SXSW 2021 and Y-Oslo 2021.
